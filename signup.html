  • Phone Number: Enter a 10-digit mobile number (e.g., 9876543210).
  • Password:
    • At least 8 characters
    • Include uppercase & lowercase letters
    • Include numbers & symbols (e.g., @, #, $)
  • Confirm Password: Must match the password exactly.
